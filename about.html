About The Restaurant

Welcome To KMenz Restaurant! We are the best and friendliest restaurant in Asawasi, Kumasi. When looking for a place to eat with full relaxation and harmony, then look no further than KMenz Restaurant!

Brief History of KMenz Restaurant

Kelvin Owusu Mensah officially started KMenz Restaurant when he turned 30, back in 1980. He rented a roadside kiosk at Aboabo, Kumasi, where he started serving roasted sausages.

This business succeeded in time resulting in about two hundred branches all over the world.
